Faith Evangelical Free Church

Welcome

Our desire is to love God and to love others and we welcome you to our family! We hope you will find Faith EFC to be a place where you can learn and embrace God's Truth. We are a Christ-centered, Bible-believing church, affiliated with the Evangelical Free Church of America. Our worship includes traditional hymns as well as contemporary music with a scriptural-based message.
